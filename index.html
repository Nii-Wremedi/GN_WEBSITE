About GN Group

GN Group brings together decades of expertise in petroleum transport through GN Incorporated and a broad range of construction and business ventures through GN Ventures Inc.. From safe and efficient haulage services to cutting-edge construction projects, our mission is to deliver excellence, foster trust, and build lasting relationships across Liberia and West Africa.

Our Subsidiaries

GN Incorporated

Established in 2008, GN Incorporated is a trusted name in petroleum transport and haulage across Liberia. With an unwavering focus on safety, compliance, and operational efficiency, we proudly serve partners like Conex Petroleum Group. Our goal is to set the standard for reliable fuel logistics in the region.

Learn More

GN Ventures Inc.

Founded in 2024, GN Ventures Inc. is a multi-sector company specializing in construction, equipment supply, real estate, and more. From building roads, bridges, and apartments to providing mining, waste management, and hospitality services, we deliver projects that drive sustainable growth and development across Liberia and West Africa.
